Savory Chicken Stir-Fry

A quick, delicious, and healthy meal perfect for any night of the week.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb chicken breast, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 1 cup broccoli florets
  • 1 cup carrots, sliced
  • 1 cup bell peppers, sliced
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp ginger, minced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 1 tbsp cornstarch
  • 2 tbsp water

Instructions

  1. In a small bowl, whisk together soy sauce, ginger, garlic, cornstarch, and water. Set aside.
  2. Heat vegetable oil in a large skillet or wok over medium-high heat.
  3. Add the chicken to the hot skillet and stir-fry until cooked through and lightly browned.
  4. Add broccoli, carrots, and bell peppers to the skillet. Stir-fry for 5â€“7 minutes, until the vegetables are tender-crisp.
  5. Pour the prepared sauce over the chicken and vegetables. Stir to coat evenly and cook for another minute or two until the sauce thickens.
  6. Serve immediately over steamed rice or noodles. Enjoy!
